Privacy policy

How Game Screen handles your data

Updated on 20 March,2024

Introduction

Welcome to Game Screen! Our platform is dedicated to providing you with an immersive gaming experience, and we value your privacy and security. This privacy policy outlines how Game Screen handles data and ensures the privacy of its users. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we manage your information.


Data Collection

Game Screen does not collect any personal data such as names, email addresses, or any other identifiable information from its users. The only data we save is related to saved wallpapers, which is stored locally on your browser. This data can be easily deleted by clearing your site data through your browser settings.


Data Usage

The data related to saved wallpapers is solely used for your convenience, allowing you to access your saved wallpapers across sessions. We do not use this data for any other purposes, such as personalization or analytics, nor do we share it with any third parties.


Data Security

Game Screen prioritizes the security of your data. As the data related to saved wallpapers is stored locally on your browser, it is protected by your browser's security measures. We do not have access to or control over this data.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is subject to updates and modifications. Any changes will be communicated through our website. It is your responsibility to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle your data.


Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or data practices, please feel free to contact us through our website.
